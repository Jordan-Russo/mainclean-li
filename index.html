About MaidClean LI

For over 30 years, MaidClean LI has been your trusted partner in maintaining clean and healthy living spaces. Our journey began in 1992 when we set out to provide top-notch cleaning services in Long Island, NY.

Over the decades, we've had the privilege of serving countless families and businesses, helping them enjoy the benefits of a clean and well-maintained environment. We are proud to have been a part of the Long Island community, and our commitment to excellence remains as strong as ever.

At MaidClean LI, we believe that a clean home or workspace is more than just a physical space; it's where life happens. It's where families bond, where memories are made, and where businesses thrive. We understand the importance of creating a clean and comfortable environment that allows you to focus on what truly matters.

Our team of dedicated cleaning professionals brings a wealth of experience and a passion for exceeding your expectations. We are driven by the satisfaction of our customers and are committed to maintaining the highest standards of integrity and professionalism.

We look forward to serving you for the next 30 years and beyond, making your spaces shine and your life more enjoyable.
